Our responsibility

Good for you.
Good for the planet.

Sustainability is not a marketing buzzword for us — it is an operational requirement we set for ourselves every day, from raw ingredient to recycling.

♻️  Packaging

Our cans are made from 100% recycled aluminium. Aluminium can be recycled indefinitely without losing quality — it is the best option for carbonated drinks.

💧  Water

We source our spring water from a certified groundwater reservoir in Dalarna. Consumption is measured every quarter and we offset usage by supporting local water conservation projects.

🌿  Ingredients

All fruit and herb extracts come from organically certified producers, primarily within Scandinavia. We publish our full supplier list on request.

🚚  Logistics

Deliveries within Sweden are made by electric vehicles from our terminal in Enköping. For the rest of the Nordics we partner with DHL GoGreen and offset remaining emissions via Gold Standard-certified projects.

🏭  Production

Our Enköping facility runs on 100% renewable electricity (wind power via Bixia). Residual heat is recovered and used to heat adjacent premises.

📊  Transparency

We publish an open sustainability report every year. Questions about our environmental impact? Reach out at sustainability@brusdrycker.se.
