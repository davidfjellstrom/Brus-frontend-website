Our story

Water should
taste like something.

BRUS was founded in 2021 by two old friends who got tired of sugary soft drinks but wanted something more exciting in their glass than tap water.

What we believe in

No compromises on ingredients. We only use natural flavours and extracts from certified producers. Our spring water is sourced from the Dalälven watershed.

Today

We are a team of nine based in Stockholm. Production takes place at our facility in Enköping and we distribute across the Nordics — straight to your door.

Our values

Honesty in everything we do — towards customers, producers and the planet. We publish an open sustainability report every year and welcome questions at hej@brusdrycker.se.
